The Rules

How It Works

Pay to Enter

One-time entry fee of 0.005 ETH. That's your ticket into the season. The moment you enter, you're a target.

Survive the Reaper

Every day for 14 days, the Reaper randomly kills a percentage of the player base. The rate escalates from 3% on Day 1 to 30% on Day 14. Nobody is safe.

Kill or Hide

Spend ETH to attempt kills on other players. More ETH = better odds, but it's never guaranteed. Or spend nothing, hide, and pray. Both are valid strategies.

Survivors Split the Pot

When the season ends, every player still alive splits 70% of the total death pool. Every entry fee, every kill attempt, every failed assassination — it's all in there.

The Weapon

The Kill Curve

Spending more ETH on a kill attempt increases your odds — but it follows a logarithmic curve. The first 0.01 ETH buys you a lot. After that, the returns diminish hard. And the cap? 65%. Even if you throw 1 ETH at someone, there's a 35% chance they walk away laughing — and your ETH joins the pool for everyone else.

There are no guaranteed kills. The Reaper always has the last word.

The Meta

Two Ways to Play

The Predator

Spend ETH. Hunt targets. Stack kills. Climb the leaderboard. Earn "The Butcher" title. Your kills thin the herd and increase YOUR payout — if you survive. Every kill attempt adds to the pool, so even your misses fund the endgame. High risk, high glory.

The Ghost

Pay 0.005 ETH. Do nothing. Pray. If the Reaper skips you for 14 days, you split a pot funded by everyone else's violence. Some of the biggest ROIs will come from players who never touched the kill button. There's a title for it: "The Ghost."

Both strategies are valid. Both can win. That's the game theory.
